Shin Wolford

Shin Wolford is the main character of Kenja no Mago. After dying in a car accident. He was reincarnated into an unknown world carrying with him a vague memory of his past life in Japan. He is the leader of Ultimate Magic Research Society and ranks 1st in the first year S-Class.

Author: Wiki

Laxus Dreyar

Laxus Dreyar is the 23-year-old S-Class mage of Fairy Tail guild. He is the grandson of Fairy Tail's master, Makarov Dreyar, and son of Ivan Dreyar. Laxus is a master of Lightning magic. It allows him to produce, control and manipulate lightning and electricity at his will.

Author: myanimelist

Cid Kagenō

Cid Kagenō, formerly Minoru Kagenō, is the titular antihero and main protagonist of The Eminence in Shadow series. He is the leader of the secret organization Shadow Garden under the moniker Shadow, which he founded to live out his chūnibyō fantasies, unaware that his imaginary enemies actually exist.

Author: Fandom

Fuyumi Irisu

Fuyumi Irisu (入須 冬実 Irisu Fuyumi) is a secondary character of Hyouka. She is a second-year student of the Kamiyama High School and director of an independent film produced by Class 2-F. She is quite famous in school - her family runs the city hospital in Kamiyama. Many students call her "The Empress".

Author: Fandom

Natsu Dragneel

Natsu Dragneel is the main male protagonist of the series and a member of the Fairy Tail Guild. He is frequently partnered with fellow Fairy Tail members Happy, Lucy Heartfilia, Gray Fullbuster, and Erza Scarlet. He is commonly known to the public as a powerful mage called "The Salamander." But this title has started to fade as the series progresses.

Author: myanimelist

Cmdr. Erwin Smith

Commander Erwin Smith, a pivotal supporting protagonist in Attack on Titan, is a serious and calculating man. He blends eloquent speech with brilliance and ruthlessness, all in his quest for truth, while caring deeply for his comrades and making heavy sacrifices.

Author: Govind S Nair

Doraemon

Doraemon is a fictional character in a Japanese manga and anime series. The series is about a robotic cat named Doraemon who travels back in time from the 22nd century to help a young boy named Nobita improve his life.

Author: Adithya S Nair

Monkey D. Luffy

The charismatic and adventurous pirate captain in "One Piece," boasts rubber-like abilities from a Devil Fruit. With his straw hat and dream of becoming the Pirate King, Luffy leads the Straw Hat Pirates on a thrilling quest for the legendary treasure, the One Piece, known for his unwavering determination and loyalty to his crew.

Author: Ram Sankar S
